The CCC (Complete Creamy Carbonara)

Description

This is my go-to meal. You get a lot of proteins, you get a lot of lipids, you get a lot of carbs, but more importantly: you get a lot of flavour

Cheap as hell, quick as Sonic and tasty as your crush, it's the complete carbonara of a life

Ingredients

  • 100g of pasta
  • 50g of pancetta, or guanciale
  • 100g of chicken breast
  • 100g of heavy cream
  • 50g of parmesan or peccorino
  • 1 white onion
  • 1 shallot
  • 100g of mushrooms

Steps

  1. In a pan, start cooking the pancetta on medium heat, without oil
  2. When the pancetta has rendered all its fat, take it off and cook thee onion and the shallot in the resting fat
  3. Cut the chicken breast and the mushrooms in small pieces
  4. Add the chicken breast and the mushrooms to the pan, and start as well to cook the pasta in boiling water
  5. When the chicken breast is cooked, add back the pancetta in the pan, the heavy cream, and cook on medium-low heat
  6. Add the cooked pasta and the parmesan in the pan
  7. Mix well everything, and serve!
