Chicken and vegetables

Description

Welcome to the recipe for my famous easy tasty chicken

Quick, cheap and a lots of proteins

Ingredients

  • 200g of chicken
  • 100 to 200g of chosen vegetables
  • 10ml of oil
  • spice

Steps

  1. Start by heating your oil in a pan
  2. Add your chicken until it starts to brown
  3. Add your vegetables and your spices and let it cook
