The Turkey Udon Noodles

Description

Why eat instant ramen when you can cook these delicious udon noodles, stacked with proteins thanks to the turkey breast ? Well, maybe because there aren't instant...

But they are still quick to prepare! And even quicker to eat

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of Udon Noodles sous-vide
  • 150g of turkey breast (or chicken breast)
  • 1 garlic
  • 1 onion
  • 3 shallots
  • 1 green onion
  • 200g of lettuce
  • 10g of nuts

For the sauce

  • 2 tbs of soy sauce
  • 2 tbs of oyster sauce
  • 2 tsp of sesame oil
  • 1 tsp of brown sugar
  • 1 tsp of MSG

Steps

  1. Chop and add into a bowl the garlic, onion, shallots and the green onion (separate the white and green parts)
  2. Add them to a mid-high heating wok
  3. While it's cooking, cut the turkey breast in small pieces
  4. Add the turkey breast to the woke, and start as well the cooking of the Udon Noodles in hot water (not boiling)
  5. While everything is cooking, chop the lettuce and prepare the sauce
  6. Shortly before the noodles are cooked, add the lettuce to the wok, and bring down the heat to low-medium
  7. Drain and add the noodles to the wok, as well as the sauce
  8. Let cook a low heat for a few minutes until the sauce thickens, and add these nuts
  9. Serve in a bowl
