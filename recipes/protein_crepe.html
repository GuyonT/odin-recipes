Protein Crepe

Description

Welcome to the recipe for my famous protein crepe

The fastest way to muscle, while keeping our tastebuds alive

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 swiss yogurt
  • 50g of flour
  • 1 scoop of protein whey (~35g)
  • 20ml of milk

  • Optionnal

    • 5g of cinamon
    • 10g of sugar
    • 15g of chocolate chips
    • 15g of syrup

Steps

  1. Whisk the two eggs with the swiss yogurt
  2. Add the flour
  3. Keep mixing and then add the protein whey
  4. Add the milk
  5. Cook in a pan until the texture change
